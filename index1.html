About Us

"Food for All" is an initiative aimed at reducing food waste and ensuring equitable food distribution. Through online platforms, surplus food from households, restaurants, and events is collected and redirected to those in need. This system not only combats hunger but also promotes sustainability by minimizing food wastage.

Our Services

  • Collecting excess food from restaurants, events, and catering services
  • Sorting and repackaging food items for distribution
  • Delivering food to shelters, community centers, and individuals in need
  • Collaborating with local businesses and organizations to minimize food waste

Why It Matters

Food waste is a significant issue globally, with millions of tons of food being wasted each year while many people go hungry. By redistributing surplus food to those in need, we not only help alleviate hunger but also reduce environmental impact and promote sustainable practices.
