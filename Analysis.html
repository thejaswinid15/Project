How to Save Food

Plan meals, buy only what you need. Wrap leftovers tightly, store right (fridge for cooked, pantry for dry). Freeze extras in portions. Compost scraps to save money and the planet.

Don't Waste Food

Food waste squanders resources like water and land. It creates harmful methane gas in landfills. Many go hungry while food gets thrown away. Be mindful - save food, save money, save the planet.

Why Donation is Important

Food donations fight hunger, reducing food waste. They nourish those in need and strengthen communities. Less food in landfills helps our environment

Understanding Food Wastage

Food wastage is a significant global issue, with millions of tons of food being thrown away each year. This not only contributes to hunger and malnutrition but also has a severe impact on the environment.

One of the main reasons for food wastage is improper storage and handling, leading to premature spoilage. Lack of awareness about expiration dates and overbuying groceries also contribute to the problem.

Reducing food wastage starts with simple steps like planning meals, buying only what you need, and storing food properly. By making conscious choices and adopting sustainable practices, we can all play a part in combating food wastage and building a better future.
